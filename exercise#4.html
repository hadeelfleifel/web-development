Lorne is the head nutritionist and director at Phalanx Performance Nutrition. Lorne first became passionate about nutrition at 16 years old which led him to study for his degree in nutrition at Bournemouth university in 2007. Lorne is a former Royal Marine Commando and has raced up to an Elite level in triathlon. He has represented the Royal Navy in the pool winning gold medals as well as playing in international water polo fixtures. In the last 3 years Lorne has switched his attention to strength sports and has reached a podium in competitive strongman events.
